RASHMIKA MANDANNA

RASHMI-CARRIES

Rashmika Mandanna started her career as a model but because of her beauty and talent in 2016, she get a film and she debut with the Kannada movie "Kirik Party" the film become highest grossing Kannada language movie and also acting impressed everyone and she was awarded for that. After that in 2017 she get two other Kannada language movies as a lead actress in "Anjani Putra" and "Chamak". After that in 2018 she made her debut in Telugu language movies with "Chalo". But she gets popular after in 2018, worked in another Telugu movie "Geetha Govindam" with Vijay Devarakonda. Her acting performances movies lover and critics both love won awards and also she gains a fan following. The same year she gets another Telugu movie "Devadas". In 2019 Kannada movie "Yajamana" film become a superhit and same year in Telugu movie "Dear Comrade" become a blockbuster. Rashmika Mandanna establish herself as a Telugu actress because she worked in Telugu movies more than others. In 2020 she worked for the first time with "Mahesh Babu" in "Sarileru Neekevvaru". After that in 2021, she made her debut in the Tamil movie with "Sulthan" co-star Karthi. The same year she worked Telugu movie "Pushpa: The Rise" with Allu Arjun and she get more popularity from that movie. In 2022 Rasgmika Mandanna made her Bollywood Hindi movie debut with "Goodbye", the film not working but in 2023, already her two movies were released Tamil movie "Varisu" with Thalapathy Vijay, and another "Mission Majnu" a Netflix movie with Sidharth Malhotra and some her big-budget movies coming in 2023.
